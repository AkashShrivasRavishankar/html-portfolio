Top 3 animes according to me as of now

Here are some of my favourite animes currently

Hunter x Hunter

A show which appears to be a kids show turns dark and thrilling which impressed me.

Grand Blue Dreaming

This one is for the boys. A show that inspires you to live your life with glee.

Tokyo Revengers

Before starting the show, I felt gang stuff is very boring. This show explored romance, time travel and bromance all at once which impressed me.
